In the twilight's gentle glow, where dreams take flight,
The stars whisper secrets through the veil of night.
Their light dances softly on the silent streams,
As moonbeams weave tales in the realm of dreams.

Each breath of wind carries a whispered rhyme,
Echoes of ancient tales lost in time.
The world slows down, a tranquil, peaceful sight,
In the hush of evening, everything feels right.

Amidst the shadows, where mysteries lie,
There's a magic that sparkles in every sigh.
A symphony of silence, a poet's delight,
In the quiet of night, the soul takes flight.
